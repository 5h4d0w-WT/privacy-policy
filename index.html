Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Humanity Chat collects, uses, stores, and protects user information. By using Humanity Chat, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Humanity Chat may collect the following information:

  • User ID (Human ID)
  • Messages and other content you create inside the app
  • Information related to voluntary donations processed through PayPal

We do not require users to provide an email address or their real name to use the application.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide chat functionality.
  • Maintain user accounts.
  • Protect the platform against abuse, harassment, spam, and fraudulent activity.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. Data Retention

  • Public chat messages are automatically deleted from our database after approximately 10 hours.
  • Private chat messages are automatically deleted from our database after approximately 1 hour.

Certain information may be retained when necessary for legal obligations, security investigations, abuse prevention, or compliance with applicable laws.

4. Payments

Donations are processed securely through PayPal. Humanity Chat does not store your payment card information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties except where required to provide services (such as PayPal for payment processing) or when required by law.

6. Security

Data transmitted between the application and our servers is encrypted using secure HTTPS connections. We continuously work to protect user information against unauthorized access.

7. Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account by following the instructions available on our Account Deletion page.

Deleting an account does not necessarily remove all previously sent messages when retention is required for safety, abuse prevention, legal obligations, or ongoing investigations.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:
